Design Choices

Chosen Typefaces - Using both Serif and Sans Serif Fonts makes text easily distinguishable.
Colour Pallete - Dark Background to reduce glare in the dim lighting of a bar.
Large Buttons, Simple Text and Layout - Increases Comprehension and Accessibility.
Logical Linear Progression - A sense of spatial awarenss makes it less likely for the user to get lost.
